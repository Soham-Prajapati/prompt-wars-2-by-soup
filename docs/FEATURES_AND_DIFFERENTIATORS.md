## Key Differentiators

1. **Constituency-First Civic Intelligence**
   - Most products stay generic; ElectIQ starts from "your seat, your voter reality."

2. **Explain + Verify + Simulate in One Loop**
   - Education (chat), truth validation (fact-check), and confidence-building (EVM sim) in one product flow.

3. **Neutrality as Product Behavior**
   - Source-backed answers, non-partisan framing, and visible trust markers are defaults.

4. **Inclusion by Design**
   - Simple language path, multilingual direction, and low-friction web-first access before full mobile parity.
